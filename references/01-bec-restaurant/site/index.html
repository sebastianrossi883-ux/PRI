About us

Paris, Villasimius, Montreal, hotels and stars, Sydney, Luxembourg, eventually THE meeting, London and finally Paradou. Then there was Paris again and a new life changing meeting  in The Baux de Provence !

What's our story

We wish to meet and greet you in the humblest and simplest way we know how for you to try our cuisine, share experiences and spend a nice moment around a table.

Back in 2019, there were a “Mas from Provence”, two passionate people, experience, know-how a perfect cocktail for Jessica and Edouard to express their common vision of a restaurant: good vibe, simplicity, gourmandise.

7 years later, yet another encounter signed a turning point for bec restaurant Paradou. Quentin Lailler joined the team to lead the kitchen team as head Chef, following Edouard’s path.

In 2026, there still is a “Mas from Provence”, with now three passionate people to meet, greet and please you around our tables.
